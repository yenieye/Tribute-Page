A Tribute to Lang Leav

The woman who is known for her best selling novels and poetry collection

The story of Lang Leav

  • Born on September 8, 1980
  • Born at a refugee camp in Thailand
  • Her family fled from Khmer Rouge regime in Cambodia
  • In 1981, they migrated to Australia
  • She was raised at a refugee town in Cabramatta, Sydney
  • She attended the College of Fine Arts in Sydney
  • Contemporary poet and writer based in New Zealand

Awards and Recognitions

  • Winner of Wantas Spirit of Youth Award
  • Scholar of Churchill Fellowship
  • Goodreads Reader's Choice Award

Poetry Collection

  • Love and Misadventures (2013)
  • lullablies (2014)
  • Memories (2015)
  • The Universe of Us (2016)
  • Anthology of Love (2017)
  • Sea of Strangers (2018)
  • Love looks pretty on you (2019)
  • September Love (2020)

Novels

  • Sad Girls (2017)
  • Poemsia (2019)
